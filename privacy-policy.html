Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 08, 2025

At Amphoteric Ltd ("we", "our", "us") we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our video games and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Data: Progress, scores, achievements, and game statistics
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, unique device ID
  • Usage Data: Playtime, features used, frequency of use
  • Performance Data: Errors, crashes, diagnostic information

1.2 Information Collected With Your Consent

  • Account information (if you register)
  • Profile data (if you provide it)
  • Communications with us

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our games and services
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Analyze usage and performance of our games
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With companies that help us operate our services (analytics, hosting, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you expressly authorize us

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google Play Services: For distribution and analytics
  • Analytics Services: Such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising Services: If we include advertising in our games

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable legislation, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Request data portability

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you about significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and, if necessary, through other means.
